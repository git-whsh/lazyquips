Reserved project identity includes:

- Official bundle identifiers used by official distribution, including `com.lazyquips.app` and `com.lazyquips.app.login-helper`.

Do not imply that a fork, modified build, package, or release is an official Lazy Quips build unless it is published by the project owner through an official channel.
